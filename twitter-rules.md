# Twitter/X Content Rules

## Last Updated
2026-03-14

## Emoji/Smiley Policy
**NO EMOJIS IN TWEETS**

Examples:
- ❌ "🚀 Thread sur..."
- ✅ "THREAD: ..."

- ❌ "💰 $10K MRR"
- ✅ "$10K MRR"

- ❌ "🔥 Les 10..."
- ✅ "Les 10..."

- ❌ "👇 Check this"
- ✅ "Check this"

Allowed punctuation for emphasis:
- CAPS for important words
- **Bold** (if platform supports)
- "..." for suspense
- "!" for excitement (sparingly)

## Other Rules
- Tag relevant accounts when available
- Include source links when applicable
- Keep thread format: Intro + numbered tweets + conclusion
- Maximum 280 chars per tweet
